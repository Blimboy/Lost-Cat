What is Dog-Gone?

It started when I was looking for an ID Tag for our dogs, I wanted one that would allow us to be contacted if they were found.

There are quite a few already on the market, but it seemed that they were all more interested in my details than my dogs! They all wanted me to pay a subscription or to sign up to their app.

I just wanted someone to contact me if they find my dog!

Dog-Gone QR Code

The Dog-Gone QR code can be engraved into a metal tag to be attached to your dogs collar or harness.

When the QR Tag is scanned, the following screen will be displayed on the finders device.

The finder can then choose to call you on the number(s) displayed or they can click the button to send you an SMS including their current GPS location.

The only details required are, your contact number(s) and your dogs name.
